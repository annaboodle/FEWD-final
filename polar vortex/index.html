Frostbite!

Polar Vortex

COULD IT BE? The nefarious polar vortex, back to strike again?

The only way to avoid freezing to death is to keep up your body heat - and make sure you avoid those razor-sharp snowflakes!

Use the arrow keys to move, and press the spacebar to begin your journey. If you're hit by three snowflakes, the vortex has won. And we all know what that means.
